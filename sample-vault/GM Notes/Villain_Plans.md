# The Compact's Endgame

> **This note is inside `GM Notes/` — excluded by folder, not by front-matter.**
> **Demonstrates that folder exclusion suppresses notes even without `gm-only: true`.**

## Long-Term Plan

The Thornwall Compact intends to:

1. Assassinate the Duke during the Harvest Festival.
2. Install a puppet mayor (Aldric is already compromised).
3. Seize control of the tunnel network beneath the city.
4. Use the tunnels to smuggle Void Crystals out to their buyers in the Free Cities.

## Key Agents

| Agent | Role | Location |
|---|---|---|
| Lady Maren | Council head | Castle keep |
| Zorn Blackthorn | Enforcer / fence | The Lower Warrens |
| Brother Aldane | Intelligence | Temple of the Allfather |

## Contingencies

- If the party discovers the tunnels → Compact triggers the false arrest warrant.
- If Aldric breaks → Lady Maren will have him killed and blame the party.
- If the party reaches Lady Maren → she offers them a deal (campaign divergence point).

## Campaign End Conditions

- **Victory (Good):** expose the Compact before the Harvest Festival; Aldric testifies.
- **Victory (Neutral):** defeat the Compact militarily; city destabilised for 1d6 years.
- **Failure:** the Duke is assassinated; the Compact takes control; campaign shifts to resistance arc.
