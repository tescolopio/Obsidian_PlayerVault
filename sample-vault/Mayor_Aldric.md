# Mayor Aldric Vane

**Full name:** Aldric Vane  
**Title:** Lord Mayor of Thornwall  
**Age:** 61  
**Appearance:** Silver-haired, heavy-set, wears the mayoral chain with obvious pride.

## Public Persona

Mayor Aldric projects the image of a steady, cautious administrator. He has held office for twelve years and is widely regarded as competent if uninspiring.

%%SECRET%%
## True Nature

Aldric is a **deep-cover agent** of the Circle of the Hollow Moon — a secretive order that believes Thornwall was built atop a sealed planar rift. He has been slowly redirecting city funds to pay for excavations beneath the keep.

He is not malevolent; he genuinely believes opening the rift will usher in an age of prosperity. He is wrong.

His handler is a figure known only as **the Archivist**, whose identity is revealed in [[GM_Session_Notes]].
%%SECRET%%

## Relationship to the Party

The mayor will initially hire the party as "independent troubleshooters" to look into the lights seen in [[The Dark Forest]]. He is friendly and pays well.

> [!gm-only] Perception / Insight notes
> A DC 15 Insight check during any meeting with Aldric reveals that he is visibly anxious whenever the sewers or the keep's sub-levels come up in conversation.
> A DC 12 Perception check in his office reveals faint chalk-dust on his hands and a map of the old city's foundations partially tucked beneath a ledger.
