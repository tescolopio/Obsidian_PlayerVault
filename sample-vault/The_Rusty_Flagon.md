# The Rusty Flagon

The Rusty Flagon is the oldest inn in the Lower Warrens. It smells of woodsmoke and cheap ale, and the floorboards creak in a pattern that regulars claim tells the weather.

## Rooms & Rates

| Room | Beds | Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Common room | 12 mats | 2 cp/night |
| Private room | 1 bed | 5 sp/night |
| The Loft | 1 large bed | 1 gp/night |

## Staff

- **Marta** — innkeeper, mid-50s, no-nonsense
- **Pip** — barkeep and Marta's nephew

> [!gm-only] Hidden Room
> Behind the fireplace is a hidden door (Perception DC 14) that leads to a smugglers' passage connecting to the docks. Marta knows about it and will deny it unless the party earns her trust (Persuasion DC 18 after at least one long rest at the inn).
>
> The passage is also used by [[Zorn Blackthorn]] for his deliveries.

## Rumours (public)

Every night, roll 1d4 and drop one of these hooks in conversation:

1. "They say [[Mayor Aldric]] hasn't left the keep in a week."
2. "Strange lights in [[The Dark Forest]] again last night."
3. "The merchant guild is raising dock fees again."
4. "Someone found a body at the old mill — guards are keeping it quiet."
