# The Ancient Prophecy of the Hollow Moon

*Translated from a pre-Compact arcanist's journal, circa 350 AE*

---

> When the Veil wears thin and the moon hangs hollow,  
> the Thornwall shall crack and the deep light shall follow.  
> What was sealed shall open; what was lost shall return.  
> The city that forgot shall finally learn.

---

## Scholarly Notes

The prophecy is recorded in at least three independent sources, all dating to within twenty years of each other. Scholars disagree on whether "the Thornwall" refers to the city or to a literal wall of some kind.

%%SECRET%%
## GM Interpretation

The prophecy refers to the planar rift beneath [[Thornwall]]. "The moon hangs hollow" is an astronomical event (a total lunar eclipse) that occurs next in approximately **27 days** from the campaign start date — creating a hard deadline for the players.

[[Mayor Aldric]] has decoded this and is working to open the rift *before* the eclipse rather than after, believing the eclipse is the danger rather than his own meddling.
