# Zorn Blackthorn

**Role:** Black-market fence  
**Location:** Moves between the Warrens and the docks  
**Faction:** Independent

## What the Players Can Learn

Zorn deals in stolen goods, information, and the occasional forged document. He is not violent but will disappear overnight if he feels threatened.

He can be found at [[The Rusty Flagon]] on even-numbered nights. Ask the barkeep for "a dark ale and a candle" as a pass-phrase.

%%SECRET%%
## What the Players Cannot Learn (Yet)

Zorn is a former Hollow Moon cultist. He left the order three years ago when he witnessed what the rift does to living creatures. He now keeps quiet out of fear of [[Mayor Aldric]]'s agents.

He will share everything he knows if the party rescues his daughter from the cultist holding house in the Warrens (see [[GM_Session_Notes]] — Session 4).
%%SECRET%%

## Stats

Zorn uses **Spy** statistics (MM p.349) with Thieves' Tools proficiency added.
