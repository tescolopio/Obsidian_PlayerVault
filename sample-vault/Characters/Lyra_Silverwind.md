# Lyra Silverwind

**Role:** Captain of the Thornwall Merchant Guild  
**Location:** Guild Hall, Merchant Quarter  
**Faction:** Merchant Guild (neutral)

## Personality

Lyra is sharp, practical, and fiercely protective of the guild's autonomy. She distrusts [[Mayor Aldric]] on general principle and has her own network of informants across the city.

## Roleplaying Notes

She speaks quickly and rarely makes eye contact, preferring to study paperwork while talking. She respects directness and despises flattery.

%%GM%%
Lyra secretly knows about the tunnel network beneath [[The Rusty Flagon]] — she uses it to move untaxed shipments. She will become an ally if the party keeps this quiet, a dangerous enemy if they expose her.

Her informant inside the keep is a scullery maid named Bessa (commoner stats, Deception +4).
%%GM%%

## Hook

She will approach the party after their first meeting with [[Mayor Aldric]] and offer a counter-deal: bring her proof of what the mayor is excavating beneath the keep, and she'll pay triple whatever he's offered.
