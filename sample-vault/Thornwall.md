# The City of Thornwall

Thornwall is an ancient walled city that has stood for over four hundred years.

%%SECRET%%
A secret network of tunnels runs beneath the city. The entrance is hidden behind the fireplace in [[The Rusty Flagon]]. The players can discover this in Session 6.
%%SECRET%%

## Districts

The city is divided into three districts:

- **The Upper Ring** — noble estates and the keep
- **The Merchant Quarter** — guilds, the Grand Market, and the bank
- **The Lower Warrens** — tenements, the docks, and the Flagon

%%GM%%
Random encounter table for the Warrens at night:
1. 1d4 pickpockets (CR 1/8 each)
2. Drunk city guard — bribeable for 5 gp
3. A wounded courier carrying sealed letters for [[Mayor Aldric]]
4. Nothing — but the players feel watched
%%GM%%

## History

The city was founded by the Thornwall Compact in 412 AE. Its walls have never been breached.

## Key Figures

- [[Mayor Aldric]] — the current mayor
- [[Lyra Silverwind]] — captain of the merchant guild
- [[Zorn Blackthorn]] — black-market fence
