## The Archivist

The Archivist's true identity is **Sister Maren Voss**, the head librarian of the Grand Library. She has been building the Hollow Moon's knowledge base for thirty years from inside the city's center of learning.

She carries a sending stone keyed to [[Mayor Aldric]]'s matching stone.
